High-energy songs are often good for workouts or parties because they have strong rhythm and fast tempo.

Chill and lofi songs are better for studying or relaxing because they have lower energy and softer sounds.

Rock and intense songs usually feel powerful due to high energy and aggressive instruments.

Acoustic songs feel softer and more natural because they use fewer electronic elements.

Energy determines how exciting or calm a song feels.

Mood captures the emotional tone of a song.
